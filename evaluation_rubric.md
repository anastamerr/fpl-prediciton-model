# LLM Qualitative Evaluation Rubric (1-5 per dimension)

Score each response on the seven dimensions below. Use whole numbers only.

- Answer Quality: Completeness and usefulness for the userâ€™s FPL need.
- Relevance: Focus on the asked question without digressions.
- Naturalness: Fluency and readability of the text.
- Correctness: Factual alignment with provided KG context.
- Instruction Following: Adherence to persona and constraints (no hallucination).
- Explanation Quality: Clarity of reasoning/justification when applicable.
- FPL Domain Knowledge: Demonstrates understanding of FPL concepts and terminology.

Guidance:
- 5 = Excellent, 4 = Good, 3 = Adequate, 2 = Weak, 1 = Poor.
- Note strengths/weaknesses per model and per query; highlight hallucinations explicitly.
